§ 01

A short introduction

I live in Chicago. I'm working toward teaching high school biology in the public school system, which means I spend a lot of time at community college, a fair amount thinking about science literacy, and a small but persistent amount drafting a constitution for a republic that doesn't exist.

I write science fiction. Sometimes I publish it. More often I rewrite it. I read constantly, I cook with my wife on weeknights, and I am of the strong opinion that No Man's Sky is the greatest video game ever made and that this is not a controversial position.

This site is a hub, not a destination. The work is the work.
